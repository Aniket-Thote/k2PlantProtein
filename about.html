About Us

The story behind Proteen Powder and our commitment to quality.

Our Story

Proteen Powder was born from a simple idea: create a plant-based protein supplement that actually tastes good and delivers real results.

Founded in 2020 by a team of nutrition scientists and fitness enthusiasts, we set out to solve the common problems with protein supplements - poor taste, digestive issues, and questionable ingredients.

After two years of research and development, countless taste tests, and rigorous scientific validation, we're proud to bring you what we believe is the most effective, best-tasting plant protein on the market.

Our Mission

At Proteen Powder, our mission is to make premium nutrition accessible to everyone while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and transparency.

We believe that what you put in your body matters. That's why we:

  • Source only the highest-quality, sustainable ingredients
  • Formulate based on scientific research, not trends
  • Test every batch for purity and potency
  • Provide complete transparency about our ingredients and processes
  • Use eco-friendly packaging and sustainable practices
