Product Description

Proteen Powder is a revolutionary plant-based protein supplement designed for athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone looking to increase their protein intake with clean, sustainable ingredients.

Unlike conventional protein powders that often rely on animal products or contain artificial additives, our formula harnesses the power of premium plant proteins, creating a complete amino acid profile that rivals whey protein in effectiveness, but without the digestive discomfort many experience with dairy-based supplements.

Available in three delicious flavors (Vanilla Bean, Rich Chocolate, and Wild Berry), Proteen Powder mixes easily with water, plant-based milk, or your favorite smoothie ingredients - no blender required.

Ingredients

We pride ourselves on using only the highest quality, traceable ingredients in our formula. Here's a breakdown of what goes into our Proteen Powder:

Protein Blend (24g)

Pea Protein Isolate: Sourced from yellow split peas grown in North America, our pea protein is extracted using a water-based process that preserves its nutritional integrity. Rich in branched-chain amino acids (BCAAs), particularly arginine and lysine, this protein supports muscle recovery and growth.

Brown Rice Protein: Derived from whole grain brown rice using low-temperature processing to maintain protein quality. This hypoallergenic protein provides a complementary amino acid profile to pea protein, creating a complete protein source.

Hemp Protein: Made from organic hemp seeds, this protein adds a nutritional boost with its omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids, fiber, and antioxidants, supporting overall health and recovery.

Digestive Enzyme Blend (120mg)

Bromelain: Derived from pineapple stems, this enzyme helps break down proteins into smaller, more easily absorbed amino acids.

Papain: Extracted from papaya fruit, this enzyme further assists with protein digestion and absorption.

Protease Blend: A combination of enzymes specifically designed to optimize protein breakdown and utilization.

Natural Flavors

We use only natural flavor extracts derived from real food sources to create our delicious taste profiles. No artificial flavors, ever.

Sweetener System

Stevia Leaf Extract: A natural, zero-calorie sweetener derived from the leaves of the stevia plant.

Monk Fruit Extract: A natural sweetener from the monk fruit that adds sweetness without calories or a bitter aftertaste.

Other Ingredients

Guar Gum: A natural fiber from guar beans that improves the texture and mixability of the powder.

Sunflower Lecithin: A natural emulsifier that enhances mixability and prevents clumping.

Allergen Information

Made in a facility that also processes tree nuts. Free from dairy, soy, gluten, and artificial ingredients.

How to Use

1

Mix

Add one scoop (30g) to 8-10 oz of cold water, plant-based milk, or your favorite beverage.

Pro Tip: For best results, add liquid first, then powder.

2

Shake or Blend

Shake vigorously in a shaker bottle for 10-15 seconds or blend for 5-10 seconds until completely dissolved.

Pro Tip: Using a shaker bottle with a mixing ball will help prevent clumping.

3

Enjoy

Consume immediately for best taste and consistency.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does plant protein compare to whey protein for muscle building?

Research shows that plant proteins can be just as effective as whey for muscle building when they provide a complete amino acid profile with adequate leucine content. Our proprietary blend is specifically formulated to deliver all essential amino acids in optimal ratios for muscle protein synthesis, making it comparable to whey protein in effectiveness while being easier to digest and more environmentally sustainable.

Is Proteen Powder suitable for people with allergies?

Proteen Powder is free from common allergens including dairy, soy, gluten, and peanuts. It is manufactured in a facility that processes tree nuts, so those with severe tree nut allergies should exercise caution. Each batch is tested for allergen contamination to ensure product safety and quality.

How long will one container last?

Each container provides 30 servings (30 scoops). If you use one serving per day, a container will last one month. Many users consume 1-2 servings daily depending on their protein needs and fitness goals.

Can I use this protein powder for weight loss?

Proteen Powder can be a valuable tool in a weight management program. Protein helps increase satiety (feeling of fullness), supports lean muscle maintenance during calorie restriction, and has a higher thermic effect than carbohydrates or fats, meaning your body burns more calories digesting it. However, it should be used as part of a balanced diet and not as a sole solution for weight loss.

When is the best time to take Proteen Powder?

Proteen Powder can be beneficial at various times throughout the day. Many users find it most effective when consumed within 30 minutes after a workout to support muscle recovery. It also makes a nutritious breakfast option or between-meal snack. Some users enjoy it before bed to provide a slow release of amino acids during sleep. The best timing ultimately depends on your individual goals and schedule.
